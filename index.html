Culture

Pop culture in Honduras is expressed mostly through drawing big crowds
and having different celebrations throughout the country.
We celebrate national holidays and special events in the form of carnivals, fairs and parades.
For example, in La Ceiba the annual carnival is a week-long celebration with music, exhibitions and special food,
culminating in the most popular carnival in the country: "The Carnival of Friendship".
Being a predominatly Catholic/Christian country, it gives special attetion to celebretions such as Holy Week, Good Friday, and Christmas.
